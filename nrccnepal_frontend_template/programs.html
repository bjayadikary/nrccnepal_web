Program Overview

Research Training:Â Our esteemed program offers students the unique prospect to cultivate indispensable skills encompassing experimental design, data collection, analysis, and interpretation. Armed with these invaluable proficiencies, participants can make substantial contributions to the scientific community by generating original knowledge and addressing pertinent local challenges in domains such as healthcare, agriculture, and environmental sustainability. However, the scarcity of resources, outdated curricula, and a scarcity of research-focused institutions often impede Nepalese students from accessing adequate research training opportunities.

This program offers:

  • Expert-led sessions
  • Interactive workshops
  • Collaborative projects
  • Networking opportunities

Our Objectives

NRCC is pleased to work in synergy with a group of organizations that share the same goals. It seeks to bridge the gap between research and practice and promote an evidence-based approach to education. By encouraging research-based learning, NRCC strives to empower the next generation of Nepalese to become global leaders in education and research.

Research

Uncovering insights through rigorous exploration and analysis to contribute valuable knowledge and understanding in the research sector of Nepal by collaborative initiations.

Innovation

Pioneering creative solutions and advancements by applying research outcomes to drive positive change and progress.

Collaboration

Fostering partnerships and synergies to amplify collective strengths and address challenges in research together.
