    Nepal Research and Collaboration Center

    The Nepal Research and Collaboration Center (NRCC), founded in 2019, is dedicated to cultivating a research-driven learning environment in Nepal. Through fostering collaboration among stakeholders and advocating for research-based learning, NRCC strives to enhance educational outcomes. It supports research projects, manages interdisciplinary activities, and engages in advocacy efforts to address the challenges faced by Nepalese students, aiming to empower them with effective solutions and contribute to the advancement of education in the country. The recent initiative by NRCC includes
