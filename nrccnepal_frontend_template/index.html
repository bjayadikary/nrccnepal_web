    NRCC

    Nepal Research and Collaboration Center is a non-profit organization led by young minds to bring change in Nepal's research field. Established in 2019, NRCC has conducted various programs to create a research culture and connect people through research-based projects. NRCC strives to bring interdisciplinary fields of study under one roof through effective and collaborative learning. We aim to build a community to empower Nepalese students with effective solutions and foster the education system. Our initiatives:

    Our Objectives

    NRCC is pleased to work in synergy with a group of organizations that share the same goals. It seeks to bridge the gap between research and practice and promote an evidence-based approach to education. By encouraging research-based learning, NRCC strives to empower the next generation of Nepalese to become global leaders in education and research.

    Research

    Uncovering insights through rigorous exploration and analysis to contribute valuable knowledge and understanding in the research sector of Nepal by collaborative initiations.

    Innovate

    Pioneering creative solutions and advancements by applying research outcomes to drive positive change and progress.

    Collaborate

    Fostering partnerships and synergies to amplify collective strengths and address challenges in research together.
